    Welcome to Albatrels.

    Take control of Albatrels Academy as Jimmy Hopkins, a rebellious teenager who must navigate various challenges in a boarding school full of turmoil, where liars, cheaters, and arrogant individuals are the most renowned among students and staff. Face off against bullies from music class, win favor with teachers by helping them out, and participate in extracurricular activities outside the campus around Albatrels City—all while learning to survive the school year and ultimately becoming the ruler of the school.
